About Me

I'm Nadine El-Sherif, a HealthTech-focused Product Designer and Full-Stack Developer based in Miami, FL. I specialize in digital experiences, visual storytelling, and user-centered design — creating intuitive web and mobile products that balance visual clarity, usability, and thoughtful interaction design.

With a background that spans product design, customer experience, and now full-stack development, I bring a well-rounded perspective to every project. I've designed websites, mobile apps, prototypes, and design systems for clients across healthtech, hospitality, and AI, and I'm currently expanding my engineering skills through the MIT Professional Coding Certificate in Full Stack Development.

I care deeply about accessibility and craft, and I love the moment when a thoughtful design becomes a real, working product that people enjoy using.
